### 1. **Academic Research & Literature Reviews**

**Use Cases:**
- **Systematic Literature Reviews**: Automatically map conflicting findings across research papers
- **Meta-Analysis Preparation**: Identify studies with contradictory results for deeper analysis
- **Research Gap Identification**: Discover areas where scientific consensus is lacking
- **Interdisciplinary Research**: Bridge knowledge gaps between different academic fields

**Example Implementation:**
```
Research Topic: "Effectiveness of mindfulness meditation for anxiety treatment"
Output: Knowledge graph showing:
- Studies supporting effectiveness (high credibility: .edu sources)
- Studies showing no effect (mixed credibility)
- Methodological differences (sample sizes, control groups)
- Temporal evolution of findings (2010 vs 2023 studies)
```

**Value Proposition:**
- Reduces literature review time from weeks to hours
- Identifies methodological biases automatically
- Provides credibility-weighted synthesis of findings
- Visualizes argument topology for complex topics
